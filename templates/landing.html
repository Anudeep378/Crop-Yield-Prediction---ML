Why CropSense

Everything a smart farm needs.

From real-time climate conditions to data-driven crop suggestions — all in one lightweight, fast interface.

🧠

Random Forest Model

Trained on nationwide crop data with 200 decision trees, capturing complex non-linear patterns between climate, soil usage, and yield.

🌤️

Live Weather Integration

Automatically fetches real-time temperature, humidity, and precipitation via Open-Meteo — no API key required, always accurate.

💡

Smart Crop Suggestions

When predicted yield is low, CropSense recommends better-suited crops for your state and season based on historical yield rankings.

📍

Region-Aware Predictions

Encodes state, season, and crop with label encoders to preserve regional agricultural patterns learned during training.

⚡

Instant Results

Predictions are served in milliseconds via a lightweight Flask backend — no waiting, no heavy infrastructure.

🔓

Open & Transparent

Built on open datasets and open-source tools. The model, training pipeline, and data are fully visible and reproducible.
